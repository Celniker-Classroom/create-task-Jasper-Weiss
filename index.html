What Should I Study In College?

Background:

College majors help guide your education and shape your future career by allowing you to focus on subjects that align with your interests and strengths. By choosing a major that reflects what you enjoy, you are more likely to stay motivated and engaged in your coursework. This not only makes the learning process more enjoyable, but also helps you develop deeper knowledge and valuable skills in a specific area. Over time, these skills can open up more opportunities and lead to a more fulfilling and satisfying career path.
